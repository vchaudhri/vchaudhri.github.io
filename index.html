About

Vinay K. Chaudhri is affiliated with the Logic Group within the Computer Science Department. He is currently involved in projects on intelligent textbooks, logic education, and computational law. He was formerly a program director in the Artificial Intelligence (AI) Center at SRI International. His research focuses on the science and engineering of large knowledge base systems and spans knowledge representation and reasoning, question answering, knowledge acquisition, and innovative applications. His most recent work has been on rapidly creating knowledge bases through a combinatino of natural language processing and crowdsourcing. His teaching has included courses on knowledge graphs, logic programming and knowledge representation.
